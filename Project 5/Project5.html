PROJECT 5

Optical Illusion

This project is to create an illusional pattern that draws people into wondering how is it formed and what is the form of these shapes. Most importantly to have fun with exploring shapes and movements.

REFLECTION

When i recieved the prompt of this assignment, i knew its gonna be somethign cool. Becasue i can finally make something abstract yet cool looking and coding can make it go infinity. I looked up at a lot of layered illusion designs, a lot of them are black and white which creates different look when you stare at them for a long time. So i wanted to design something real illusional, becuse for me i think illusion is somehting you can't catch and see exactly. This is the idea i am going for and are trying to achieve in this assignment. I later went in to discovering what number would be the best so that i have a good amount to move and the speed of it would be very important since it correspond to the movement. I think my optical illusion may be happening in weaved object in my world. For instance a tightly weaved multi patterned basket or blanket if you zoom in carefully and look at it, sometimes you get similar ideas. I think my optical illusion can be incorporated into a browser extension but it might be a little extense for people since it moves very fast but i can adjust it too which rotates slower. And i think its gonna be hard to turn into a wearable piece because i am not sure how to make this animation onto something you can wear.
