This is my project 1

Lost and Found

This project is created base on my partner description of a yellow submarine toy he lost during his childhood. And I will transform human langauge to computer language by interpreting the object and the detail of it in the simplest form.

Reflection

Based on Arava's simple description of his object, I am interpreting that since this is a toy for a child at a young age then thedesign of the submarine should be cute. I am thinking the pond is green and the sky is light blue with big clouds because a child might draw everything in big scale including the submarine. So I exagerated on the size of the submarine toy, and added little bubbles pretending its actually functioning. In P5, I use a lot of shapes such as circle, rectangle, eclipse and arc. And a lot of differnt shades of colors by using the function "fill", and blending colors using "BlendMode". Another function I used to change position of the little lotus leaves is "Rotate" and "Translate", these two functions are struggling me because they must be written in the correct format! In front of everything and we can contain this movenment using "push" and "pop". I was trying to figure out the shape of the submarine during all stage because I was only given the specific color so I thought at first to create a more realistic submarine but then later I considered how kids have different perspective because they may like a different version than me so I design it more fantasy and cute.
