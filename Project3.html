PROJECT 3

Experimental Clock

This project is to create a experiment clock that can represent time in my interpretation and how does it correspond with my time. And in this case i think of time just like an hourglass because it has an certain limit and when it reaches the limit its the time.

REFLECTION

The clock that i created is novel to me because i do not look at numbers and the "clock" itself does not awares me of time. So this clock that i made are meant to be fill with awareness and colors are also telling me its out out time and once it continue in a cycle i get more anxious so i keep track of time, which makes me more faster and worried about it.

What keeps me to keep in track of time is being anxious and i think the idea of hour glass is an great example. Since there's only one side of the sand that should be falling down and ends. To me it's more helpful and scary that a time will ran out so i choose this out of my other sketches.
